Innovative Techniques in Skin Grafting: Advances and Applications

Skin grafting is a crucial surgical procedure used to treat extensive wounds, burns, and other skin-related injuries. Traditionally, skin grafts involve the transplantation of healthy skin from one part of the body to another. However, with advancements in medical technology and research, innovative techniques in skin grafting have emerged, offering improved outcomes, reduced scarring, and faster recovery times.
